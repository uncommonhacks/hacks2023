Hackathons are invention marathons where attendees collaborate on a project over the course of a weekend! Hackers will be in a fun, supportive environment with the necessary tools and mentorship provided to create weird, quirky, and amazing projects that creatively explore new ideas with code or solve unconventional problems. University and high school students from anywhere are welcome, even if you're not a computer science major or have never been to a hackathon before!
